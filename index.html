Instructions For Simon Game

How the Game Works?

The game starts when you hit any key from the keyboard

As soon as you enter any key one of the four colours will flash.Now you have to click on the flashed button.That will send you to level 2::Now again one of the 4 colours will flash.But now since you are in level 2 .You have to click 2 colours one by one.One from the previous level and one from the current Level: You actually need to remember which colour flashed in level 1,2,3,4 and so on.If you are on level i you need to click on colours from level 1 to level i.
